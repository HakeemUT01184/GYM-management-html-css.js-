BENEFITS OF REQULAR EXCERCISE


Improved Physical Health


Regular excercise can lead to
enhanced cardiovascular
health,strong muscles and
bones,improved flexibility,and
increased the risk of chronic
diseases such as heart disease,
diabetes,and certain types of
cancer.

Enhanced Mental Health


Exercise releases endorphins,
often referred to as the 'feel-
good' hormones, which can
elevate mood and reduce
feelings of stress, anxiety, and
depression. It can also improve
sleep quality and help manage
conditions such as insomnia.
